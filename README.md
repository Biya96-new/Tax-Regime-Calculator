## 📌 Problem Statement

Every year, taxpayers in India face a common challenge:

“Which tax regime should I choose?”

- The decision depends on income and deductions  
- Manual calculation is time-consuming  
- Many people end up guessing  

---

## 💡 Solution

This app simplifies the decision-making process:

- Enter your income and deductions  
- Instantly calculate tax under both regimes  
- Clearly see which option is better  

No manual calculations. No confusion.

## ⚙️ How It Works

The app is designed to make tax comparison simple and accessible.

### Step 1: Enter Your Details
Users provide:
- Gross salary  
- Other income (if any)  
- Deductions (80C, 80D, HRA, etc.)  

---

### Step 2: Tax Calculation

The app calculates tax under both regimes:

**Old Regime**
- Applies standard deduction and eligible deductions  
- Computes taxable income  
- Uses slab-based tax rates  

**New Regime**
- Uses simplified slab rates  
- Ignores most deductions  
- Applies updated tax structure  

---

### Step 3: Rebate Handling (Section 87A)

- Checks eligibility automatically  
- Applies:
  - Up to ₹5L (Old Regime)  
  - Up to ₹7L (New Regime)  

---

### Step 4: Compare Results

The app shows:
- Tax payable under both regimes  
- Difference in tax  
- Suggested better option  

This helps users quickly decide the most beneficial regime.
